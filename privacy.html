Privacy Policy

CyberSafe Local - SME Cybersecurity Risk Self-Assessment

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

CyberSafe Local ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our cybersecurity risk self-assessment application ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

Assessment Data

  • Your responses to the 12 cybersecurity assessment questions
  • Your calculated risk score and risk level
  • Assessment ID (unique identifier for your assessment)
  • Date and time of assessment completion
  • IP address (for security and logging purposes)

Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Access logs and timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver and improve the self-assessment tool
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access, fraud, and other security threats
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience (aggregated, anonymized data only)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • PDF Generation: To create downloadable assessment reports

4. Data Encryption and Security

Your assessment data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We implement multiple security measures to protect your information:

  • CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) protection on all forms
  • Rate limiting to prevent unauthorized access attempts
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS)
  • Secure cookie settings (HttpOnly, SameSite)
  • Input validation and sanitization

5. Data Storage and Retention

Assessment data is stored securely on our servers. We retain:

  • Assessment Records: For 12 months from the date of assessment, unless you request deletion
  • Access Logs: For 30 days for security and debugging purposes
  • PDF Reports: Generated on-demand; not permanently stored

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal assessment data with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your assessment data
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your assessment record and associated data
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential analytics collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our application uses essential cookies for:

  • CSRF token validation and security
  • Session management
  • User preference storage

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify individuals.

9. GDPR and Data Protection Compliance

For users in the European Union and other jurisdictions with data protection laws:

  • We process your data with explicit legal basis (performance of contract, legitimate interests)
  • We do not transfer data outside applicable regulatory regions without appropriate safeguards
  • We have implemented Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)
  • We maintain data processor agreements for any third-party service providers

10. Children's Privacy

CyberSafe Local is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take prompt action to delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the updated policy in the Service. Your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Data Breach Notification
